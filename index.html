BIO

Hi, my name is Tony Di Folca. I am a writer, Youtuber, and Twitch Affiliate Streamer. I am currently attending York University and working towards completing a degree in Information Technology. But I also work as a writer for StreamScheme.com, a content creator on YouTube, and most nights you can find me live on Twitch.tv as an affiliated streamer. As a father of two young children, it can be difficult to find the time for these passions. But, as I’ve learned from my years of experience, time management is crucial, not only in one’s professional endeavours, but in all aspects of life. It has been a long journey, taking me from the financial industry to the public transportation industry and finally settling into a career and life path that I truly love, the world of technology and content creation!

Going through the various checkpoints was extremely useful in learning how to break down larger projects like this into smaller more manageable parts. Learning how to take proper pictures for checkpoint 1 and reading the guides for it on iFixit not only taught me the basics of a useful photography skill, but showed me first hand what a technical guide should look like. Moving through to checkpoint 2 and learning more about the language of technical writing and how to use the "active voice" was instrumental in completing my own guide.
